The Information Technology Students Association first came into being in 2004 as a group of students coming together to represent the interest of the IT department and enrich their 4 years in engineering. The association has evolved over the years with numerous ideas being incorporated every year, setting milestones for the following batches to come. Now in 2016-2017 a group of 5 individuals with common interest for their department, has taken up the responsibilities of making a difference to the department that has become a part of their lives.
